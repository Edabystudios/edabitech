Top Trending Music in Nigeria 2024

The Nigerian music scene continues to shine globally, and 2024 has been no exception. From Afrobeat to Afro-fusion, Nigerian artists are dominating the airwaves and topping charts both locally and internationally. If you're a music lover in Nigeria or globally, here's a roundup of the top trending music you need to check out this year.

1. Burna Boy - "Soke" (2024 Remix)

Burna Boy has once again proven why he is one of the top African artists. "Soke," originally released a few years ago, has seen a massive remix in 2024 that is taking over the airwaves. This track blends his Afro-fusion sound with high-energy beats that make it perfect for any playlist. Whether you’re at a party or just vibing at home, "Soke" is one of the most infectious hits of 2024.

2. Wizkid - "Bad to Me" (2024 Edition)

Wizkid's contributions to global music cannot be overstated, and his latest track, "Bad to Me," is another hit that’s rocking the Nigerian and international charts. This song merges Afrobeat with smooth R&B influences, creating the perfect vibe for both daytime and late-night listening. 2024 has seen this track gain more popularity with its catchy hooks and Wizkid's signature laid-back style.

3. Davido - "Pamoja" (2024)

Davido, known for his infectious hooks and catchy rhythms, has dropped "Pamoja" – a track that combines traditional Afrobeat with modern electronic sounds. It's an anthem for parties and celebrations, with the energetic vibe and Davido's unique voice bringing the heat to every speaker. This song has become a staple on Nigerian playlists, and 2024 will surely be the year it continues to dominate.

4. Ayra Starr - "Rich Girl Anthem" (2024)

Ayra Starr's "Rich Girl Anthem" is a breakout track that speaks to the modern woman and the hustle that defines Nigerian life. Her mix of Afrobeat with pop influences has made her one of the leading female artists in Nigeria. This track has empowered many young Nigerians, making it one of the most talked-about songs of 2024. It’s a song that gets you in the mood to chase your dreams and work hard.

5. Omah Lay - "Infinity" (2024 Version)

Omah Lay is known for his ability to combine Afro-fusion with smooth melodies. "Infinity" (2024 Version) has captivated listeners with its mellow tone and introspective lyrics. It’s a track that speaks to the heart and the soul, resonating deeply with Nigerian youth. Whether you’re in a reflective mood or just enjoying a chill moment, this song remains a top trending track in 2024.

6. Joeboy - "Baby's Heart" (2024)

Joeboy is back with a bang in 2024 with his romantic anthem "Baby's Heart." Known for his high-energy songs and catchy beats, Joeboy has crafted another hit that speaks to love and connection. This song is a favorite among Nigerian teens and young adults and is making waves internationally. If you're a fan of Joeboy's brand of Afrobeat, this one is a must-add to your playlist!

7. Tems - "Lover's World" (2024)

Tems has had a monumental rise in the music industry, and "Lover's World" proves why. This track showcases her unique blend of soul, R&B, and Afrobeat influences, creating a sound that is both fresh and universally appealing. As one of Nigeria’s top exports, Tems continues to captivate audiences with her powerful vocals and emotional depth in 2024.

8. Fireboy DML - "Oxygen" (2024)

Fireboy DML has dropped yet another fire track this year with "Oxygen." This song combines romantic vibes with Afrobeat influences, and it’s quickly becoming a favorite across Nigeria. With his smooth voice and lyrical content, Fireboy continues to capture the hearts of his fans in 2024 with this beautiful ballad.

9. Zlatan - "Zanku Bop" (2024 Remix)

Zlatan is back with a remix of his hit "Zanku Bop" in 2024, and it’s been taking the streets by storm. His combination of street vibes, catchy lyrics, and infectious beats has earned him a place on the trending list once again. This track is a favorite among dancers and has become a mainstay in Nigerian clubs.

10. Rema - "Finesse" (2024 Version)

Rema’s rise to prominence has been meteoric, and "Finesse" is a testament to his ability to create hits that resonate with fans. The 2024 version of the song adds more energy, making it an even bigger hit. His ability to merge Afrobeat with other genres like trap and pop has set him apart, and this song is solidifying his place as a top artist in Nigeria for 2024.

Final Thoughts

The Nigerian music scene is thriving more than ever in 2024. From the heavyweights like Burna Boy and Wizkid to the fresh sounds of Ayra Starr and Omah Lay, Nigerian artists continue to influence global music. The songs listed here are not just trending—they’re shaping the sound of 2024, with their infectious rhythms, diverse sounds, and powerful messages. Be sure to keep these tracks on your radar as they continue to dominate the charts!
