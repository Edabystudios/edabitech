2025 JAMB is How Many Subjects?

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) exam is one of the most important exams for Nigerian students who want to gain admission into higher institutions. If you are preparing for the 2025 JAMB, you might be wondering: how many subjects will you need to take? In this article, we will break down the subject requirements for JAMB 2025 based on the course you are interested in studying.

How Many Subjects Are in JAMB 2025?

For the 2025 JAMB exam, students are required to take a total of 4 subjects. These subjects will include:

  • One (1) compulsory subject: English Language
  • Three (3) other subjects related to the course you wish to study

English Language is mandatory for all students as it is essential for admission into most degree programs in Nigerian universities. The other three subjects will be chosen based on your intended course of study.

Subject Combinations Based on Course

The subjects you will need to take for JAMB will depend on the course you want to pursue. Below are the subject combinations for some popular courses:

1. Medicine and Surgery

For this course, the recommended JAMB subject combination is:

  • English Language
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics

2. Law

For Law, the recommended subject combination is:

  • English Language
  • Literature in English
  • Government
  • CRS (Christian Religious Studies) or Economics

3. Engineering

For Engineering, the subject combination is:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

4. Accounting

The subject combination for Accounting is:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Economics
  • Government or Commerce

5. Education

For those interested in Education, the following subject combination applies:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Any of: History, Economics, Government, Geography
  • Any of: CRS, Civic Education

Can I Choose My Own Subjects?

While you have some flexibility in choosing the three elective subjects, you must ensure that the subjects align with the JAMB subject requirements for your chosen course. If you’re unsure about the correct combination for your intended course, it is advisable to check with JAMB guidelines or consult with your school of choice for confirmation.

What If I Choose the Wrong Subjects?

If you select a subject combination that is not aligned with your course, you may not be eligible for admission into your desired program. Therefore, it’s essential to carefully review the JAMB requirements for your course and select the appropriate subjects to avoid wasting time and effort.

Conclusion

For the 2025 JAMB exam, you’ll need to take 4 subjects: English Language and three others that are relevant to the course you intend to study. Make sure to plan ahead, research the right subject combinations for your chosen course, and prepare effectively for the exam. Good luck with your JAMB preparations!
