How to Upload Music via WhatsApp to 9jamix

Are you a musician or music producer looking to share your latest track with a wider audience? Uploading your music to 9jamix via WhatsApp is a quick and easy way to get your music featured. Here’s a simple guide on how you can upload your music through WhatsApp and share it with the 9jamix community.

Step 1: Prepare Your Music File

Before you send your music via WhatsApp, make sure your file is ready to go. Ensure that your track is in a supported format, such as MP3 or WAV. You can also double-check the quality of the audio to ensure there are no issues with the sound or the file.

Step 2: Save Your Music File on Your Device

Make sure the music file is saved on your phone or device in a location that is easy to access. This way, you can quickly send it through WhatsApp without any hassles.

Step 3: Add Your Music to WhatsApp

Open your WhatsApp and follow these steps to send your music:

  • Open a chat with 9jamix Music Upload on WhatsApp (+234 701 109 7887).
  • Tap the “attachment” icon (the paperclip symbol) and select the “Audio” option.
  • Choose your music file from your phone's storage.
  • Once the file is selected, click "Send" to upload it directly to our platform.

Step 4: Provide Music Details

Once the music file is sent, be sure to send the following details along with the music file:

  • Song Title: The name of the track you are submitting.
  • Artist Name: The artist(s) featured in the song.
  • Album Name: If the track is part of an album, include the album name.
  • Genre: The genre of your song (e.g., Afrobeat, Pop, Hip Hop, etc.).
  • Release Date: The date the track is officially released.
  • Cover Art (Optional): If you have an album or single cover art, send the image along (ensure it's in .jpg or .png format).

Step 5: Confirmation and Review

After you’ve sent your music and details, our team will review the submission. You’ll receive a confirmation once your track is successfully uploaded and live on 9jamix!

Step 6: Share Your Music

Once your track is live on 9jamix, it’s time to promote it! Share the link to your song on social media platforms to reach your audience, and let your fans enjoy your music. You can also interact with fans who comment on your track through the platform.

Pro Tips for a Successful Upload:

  • Make sure the audio quality is clear, with no distortion or background noise.
  • Include eye-catching cover art for your track to make it stand out.
  • Ensure that the song details you provide are accurate and complete.
  • Promote your song on social media to drive traffic and get more plays.

Need Assistance?

If you face any challenges during the submission process or need help, don’t hesitate to reach out to us at edabz@9jamix.com.ng or contact us directly via WhatsApp.
